about
osulist is an online web application that lets you easily create and share playlists of osu! beatmaps. To create a new playlist, login using the 'login' button on the top right of the website. Once logged in, click on 'new playlist' on the navigation bar.
Consider joining our Discord server if you would like to participate in anything development-related.
  • bug reports
  • feature requests
  • development discussions
  • design
osulist was developed by Feiri and other cool, lovable contributors! :D
